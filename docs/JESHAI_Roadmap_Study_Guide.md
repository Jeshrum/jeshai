# JESHAI: Autonomous Company Study Guide

This guide is designed for deep-dive reading. It consolidated the vision, the team, and the operational logic of JESHAI—your local-first AI Company Orchestrator.

---

## 🌌 The Vision: Better Than Paperclip

JESHAI is a **local-first** alternative to centralized AI orchestration layers like Paperclip. While many frameworks rely on cloud governance, JESHAI prioritizes:
1.  **Privacy**: Zero data leakage (runs via Ollama).
2.  **Efficiency**: Sequential handoffs for low-RAM performance.
3.  **Full-Stack Scope**: Technical, Business, Marketing, Finance, and Legal layers in one machine.

---

## 🏢 The Team (12 Specialized Agents)

### 💻 Technical Layer
- **Product Manager**: Requirement alignment and PRDs.
- **Architect**: System blueprinting and design patterns.
- **Frontend Engineer**: UI/UX (React, HTML, CSS).
- **Backend Engineer**: API, Databases, and Core Logic.
- **QA Engineer**: Automated testing and bug hunting.
- **Code Auditor**: Security, performance, and optimization review.
- **DevOps Engineer**: Deployment scripts and CI/CD.
- **Documentation Writer**: User manuals and API docs.

### 📈 Business & growth Layer
- **Growth Hacker**: Viral loops and user acquisition.
- **Content Strategist**: Brand voice and professional copywriting.
- **Financial Analyst**: ROI, budgets, and cost tracking.
- **Legal Officer**: Privacy compliance and ethical governance.

---

## 🛠 Skills & Autonomy

JESHAI agents are more than just "bare prompts." They are equipped with **Functional Tools**:
- **File System Control**: Read/Write files across the entire workspace.
- **Automated Hand-offs**: Context flows sequentially from PM to DevOps.
- **Terminal Execution**: Validating code by running it locally (Tests & Builds).
- **Project Onboarding**: Auditing and finishing existing legacy projects automatically.
- **Marketing Automation**: Autonomous content creation and API-driven social media management.

---

## 🏗️ Technical Mastery: The "Import & Complete" Engine
JESHAI isn't just for new ideas. It is designed to take over **unfinished projects**:
1.  **Investigative Audit**: Agents use `readFile` and `executeCommand` to map bugs.
2.  **Refactoring**: Technical agents stabilize the base.
3.  **Expansion**: PM and Architect implement the missing vision.
4.  **Launch**: Business agents take it to market.

## 💡 Key Philosophies
> [!TIP]
> **Privacy by Design**: All processing happens in RAM. Never expose your tokenomics, legal strategy, or proprietary code to a third-party API.
> **Iterative Quality**: The QA and Auditor roles act as a internal filter—nothing gets "pushed" until it passes local verification.
