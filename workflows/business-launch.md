# Business Launch Workflow

This workflow is designed to take an idea from technical blueprint all the way to a market-ready position, ensuring engineering quality and economic viability.

## 🪜 Workflow Steps

### 1. Market Alignment (Product Manager)
- **Objective**: Define the MVP (Minimum Viable Product) and target audience.
- **Handoff**: A clear product requirement document (PRD).

### 2. Strategic Blueprint (Architect)
- **Objective**: Design the technical architecture to support the PM's scope.
- **Handoff**: A system design and tech stack selection.

### 3. Core Development (Backend & Frontend)
- **Objective**: Implement the features.
- **Handoff**: Working code files in `src/`.

### 4. Quality Audit (QA & Code Auditor)
- **Objective**: Bug hunting, security review, and performance checks.
- **Handoff**: A "Certified" status or bug list for fixes.

### 5. Growth Engine (Growth Hacker)
- **Objective**: Design the acquisition plan and viral hooks for the new build.
- **Handoff**: A growth strategy and experiment list.

### 6. Public Narrative (Content Strategist)
- **Objective**: Create the landing page copy, social announcements, and README.
- **Handoff**: Marketing assets and updated project documentation.

### 7. Economic Audit (Financial Analyst)
- **Objective**: Calculate the cost of the build (token usage) and projected ROI.
- **Handoff**: A final economic health report.
